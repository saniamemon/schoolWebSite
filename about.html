URMI SCHOOL

Urmi School is one of the premier schools of Vadodara managed by BRG Group. The school started with a handful of students in the year 2002 and has now successfully branched into three flourishing campuses in Vadodara. It has marked its way as one of the elite English Medium, co-ed school of Vadodara offering alternatives of various education board curricula.

BRG Group has been in the field of education since 1992 and now manages educational institutions like GUJARAT PUBLIC SCHOOL, Atladara, Chhani, Waghodia and URMI SCHOOL & HOSTEL – Sama. We cater to the educational needs of more than 12000 students and have a staff strength of around 1200 employees – out of which 75% are females. BRG Group, since a decade has been actively involved in many Educational, Cultural, Sports, Charitable and Medical activities.

WHY US?

Excellence in education with a fine blend of activities is the motto of all our schools. We believe that children learn fast when they discover and do themselves. Here in our schools we give all prospect to discover their knowledge and get involved by doing activities themselves. Our students get National and International Level Experience, be in the field of education, media, cultural or sports.
